Video VoIP Phones

Key Features

  • Video Conferencing: High-definition video for face-to-face communication.
  • Touchscreen Displays: Intuitive controls and easy navigation.
  • Audio Quality: HD voice with noise cancellation.
  • Integration: Works with leading collaboration platforms like Zoom, Teams, and Skype.

  • Additional Features: USB ports, Bluetooth, and camera privacy shutter.
