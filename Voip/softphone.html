Softphone Applications

Key Features

  • Voice Over IP (VoIP): Use your computer or mobile device as a phone.
  • Call Management: Includes voicemail, call transfer, hold, and forwarding.
  • Integration: Compatible with CRM software and other business tools.
  • Multi-Platform Support: Works on Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android.
  • Video and Messaging: Includes video conferencing and instant messaging capabilities.

Specifications

  • Protocol: SIP or proprietary protocols.
  • Compatibility: Compatible with major VoIP service providers.
  • Audio Features: HD Voice, noise cancellation, and echo suppression.
  • Security: End-to-end encryption and secure communication protocols.
  • Additional Features: Contact syncing, call analytics, and team collaboration tools.

Price Range

Price Range: Free - $30/month per user (Subscription-Based)
