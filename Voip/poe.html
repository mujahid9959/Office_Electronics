PoE (Power over Ethernet) VoIP Phones

Key Features

  • Power over Ethernet: Eliminates the need for separate power adapters by using Ethernet cables for both power and data.
  • Cost-Efficient Setup: Simplifies cabling and reduces installation costs.
  • Reliable Performance: Ideal for consistent, high-quality voice communication.
  • Advanced Features: Supports HD voice, programmable keys, and call management tools.
  • Wide Compatibility: Works with most SIP-based and proprietary VoIP platforms.
