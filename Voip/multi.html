Multi-Line VoIP Phones

Key Features

  • Multi-Line Support: Handle multiple calls simultaneously with support for 2–12 lines or more.
  • Advanced Call Management: Features include call waiting, call forwarding, and conference calling.
  • HD Audio Quality: Crystal-clear voice with noise reduction technology.
  • Integration: Compatible with leading VoIP platforms like Zoom, Microsoft Teams, and Cisco Webex.
  • Customizable Interface: Programmable keys for quick access to frequently used functions.

Specifications

  • Lines Supported: 2–12 lines, depending on the model.
  • Display: Color touchscreen or monochrome display options.
  • Connectivity: Ethernet, Wi-Fi (in some models), and Bluetooth (optional).
  • Power Options: PoE (Power over Ethernet) and AC adapter options.
  • Compatibility: Works with SIP-based and proprietary VoIP systems.
