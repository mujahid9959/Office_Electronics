Desk VOIP Phones

Key Features

  • High-Quality Voice: Offers HD voice clarity for professional communication.
  • Multiple Lines: Supports multiple VoIP accounts and simultaneous calls.
  • Customizable Interfaces: Features like programmable keys and touchscreens for ease of use.
  • Power over Ethernet (PoE): Eliminates the need for separate power adapters.
  • Integration: Compatible with CRM and other business tools for productivity.

Specifications

  • Connectivity: Ethernet, PoE, and Wi-Fi options.
  • Display: Available in both color and monochrome LCD screens.
  • Audio Quality: HD voice, echo cancellation, and noise reduction features.
  • Compatibility: SIP and other VoIP protocols for seamless integration.
  • Expandable Features: Optional expansion modules for additional line support.
