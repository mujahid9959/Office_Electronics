Wireless VOIP Phones

Key Features

  • Wireless Connectivity: Enables users to move freely within a network's coverage area.
  • High-Definition Voice: Provides crystal-clear audio quality for calls.
  • Long Battery Life: Extended usage time for uninterrupted communication.
  • Seamless Integration: Works with existing SIP-based systems and VoIP protocols.
  • Portability: Ideal for mobile or roaming users within an office or campus.

Specifications

  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi, DECT (Digital Enhanced Cordless Telecommunications).
  • Range: Covers up to 300 meters (DECT) or as per Wi-Fi signal strength.
  • Battery Life: Up to 15 hours talk time, with quick recharging features.
  • Display: LCD or touchscreen interfaces.
  • Compatibility: SIP, VoIP, and other advanced telephony protocols.
