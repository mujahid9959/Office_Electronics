Virtual VoIP Phones

What Are Virtual VoIP Phones?

Virtual VoIP phones are software-based communication tools that allow users to make and receive calls over the internet using their computers, smartphones, or tablets. These applications replicate the functionality of a hardware VoIP phone without requiring dedicated hardware.

Key Features

  • Platform Compatibility: Available on Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android.
  • Cost-Effective: No need for additional hardware.
  • Advanced Features: Call forwarding, voicemail, conferencing, and instant messaging.
  • Integration: Works seamlessly with CRM and productivity tools.
  • HD Voice Quality: Ensures crystal-clear communication.

Specifications

  • Requirements: Internet connection and compatible device.
  • Audio/Video Support: Depends on the software.
  • Scalability: Supports single users to large organizations.
  • Customizable Interface: Tailored to user needs.
  • Security: Encryption for secure communication.

Price Range

Price Range: Free - $50/month (per user)
