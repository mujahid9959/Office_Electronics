The iPad (9th Generation) features a large 10.2-inch display that is perfect for video calls, streaming media, and reading. The A13 Bionic chip makes multitasking a breeze, and with iPadOS, you can use Split View and Slide Over to handle multiple apps at once. The front camera, with its 12MP Ultra Wide lens, features Center Stage, which automatically adjusts the framing of your face for more natural video calls.

Compatible with the Apple Pencil (1st Generation) and Smart Keyboard, the iPad (9th Generation) is perfect for students and professionals alike. With a powerful chip, an expansive display, and all the features that make it easy to stay connected and productive, the iPad (9th Generation) is a great value for anyone who needs a tablet for work or entertainment.
