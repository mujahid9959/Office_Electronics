Wi-Fi Repeater

Key Features

  • Extended Coverage: Boosts your Wi-Fi signal to cover areas with weak or no signal, improving overall network coverage.
  • Simple Setup: Easy to set up with most models offering plug-and-play functionality or simple app-based configuration.
  • Compatible with Most Routers: Works with most wireless routers, enhancing the existing network coverage.
  • Dual-Band Support: Many models support dual-band Wi-Fi (2.4 GHz and 5 GHz) for optimal performance and reduced interference.
  • Cost-Effective: An affordable solution for improving Wi-Fi coverage without needing to upgrade your router or add more access points.

Components and Specifications

  • Wi-Fi Standards: Typically supports 802.11n, 802.11ac (Wi-Fi 5), or 802.11ax (Wi-Fi 6) for faster data transfer speeds.
  • Frequency Bands: Dual-band (2.4 GHz and 5 GHz) support for better performance and fewer interferences.
  • Ethernet Ports: Some models include Ethernet ports to connect wired devices to the extended network.
  • Signal Amplification: Built-in signal amplifiers for strong and stable Wi-Fi connection over a larger area.
  • LED Indicators: For easy setup and monitoring of signal strength and performance.
