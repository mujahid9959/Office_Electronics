USB Wi-Fi Extender

Key Features

  • Easy Setup: Simple plug-and-play setup through a USB port without the need for a dedicated power outlet.
  • Increased Range: Boosts the range of your existing Wi-Fi network, eliminating dead zones and extending coverage to hard-to-reach areas.
  • Compact Design: Small and portable, making it perfect for travel or to use with a laptop, desktop, or gaming console.
  • Compatibility: Works with most Wi-Fi routers and adapters, offering support for multiple wireless standards such as 802.11n, 802.11ac, and 802.11ax (Wi-Fi 6).
  • Dual-Band Support: Many models support dual-band Wi-Fi (2.4 GHz and 5 GHz), allowing you to choose the optimal frequency for your devices.

Components and Specifications

  • Wi-Fi Standard: Typically supports Wi-Fi 5 (802.11ac), with some models supporting Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) for faster speeds and better efficiency.
  • Frequency Bands: Dual-band (2.4 GHz and 5 GHz) for optimal performance in both short-range and long-range applications.
  • USB Interface: USB 2.0 or USB 3.0 connectivity for fast and reliable data transfer between the extender and the device.
  • Speed: Speeds typically range from 300 Mbps to 1.2 Gbps, depending on the model and supported Wi-Fi standards.
  • Signal Amplification: Built-in amplifiers to extend the signal range, ensuring stable and reliable Wi-Fi connectivity.
