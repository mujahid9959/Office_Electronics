Wall-Plug Wi-Fi Extender

Key Features

  • Compact Design: Small form factor that plugs directly into a wall outlet, making it easy to install and space-saving.
  • Easy Setup: Quick installation process with most models offering a simple plug-and-play feature, often with mobile app support for setup.
  • Wi-Fi Coverage Extension: Boosts and extends your existing Wi-Fi signal to reach areas in your home or office that may have weak or no signal.
  • Dual-Band Support: Many models support both 2.4GHz and 5GHz bands for improved speed and better coverage.
  • LED Indicators: Visual indicators to help place the device in the optimal location for maximum performance.

Components and Specifications

  • Wi-Fi Standard: Typically supports 802.11ac or 802.11n standards, with some models offering Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) for faster speeds.
  • Speed: Speeds can range from 750 Mbps to 2000 Mbps depending on the model and Wi-Fi standards.
  • Ports: Some models include Ethernet ports for wired connections to devices like laptops, TVs, or gaming consoles.
  • Power Supply: Plugs directly into a power outlet, with no external power adapter needed.
  • Compatibility: Works with most Wi-Fi routers and supports a variety of devices including smartphones, laptops, and smart home appliances.
