Powerline Wi-Fi Extender

Key Features

  • Reliable Coverage: Extends your Wi-Fi signal using your existing electrical wiring for seamless internet access in hard-to-reach areas.
  • Easy Setup: Plug-and-play installation with no need for complex configurations.
  • High-Speed Connection: Offers speeds up to 1 Gbps or more, depending on the model.
  • Multiple Ethernet Ports: Some models offer additional Ethernet ports for wired connections to devices like smart TVs or gaming consoles.
  • Secure Connectivity: Supports 128-bit AES encryption to protect your network from unauthorized access.

Components and Specifications

  • Technology: Uses Powerline technology to transmit data through electrical wiring.
  • Speed: Supports various speeds from 500 Mbps to 1 Gbps or more.
  • Ports: Typically includes 1-2 Ethernet ports for wired connections and Wi-Fi for wireless devices.
  • Compatibility: Works with any router and can be used with both 2.4GHz and 5GHz Wi-Fi bands.
  • Range: Effective coverage for up to 1000 feet (depending on home layout and power line conditions).
