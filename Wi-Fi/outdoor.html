Outdoor Wi-Fi Extender

Key Features

  • Extended Coverage: Boosts your Wi-Fi signal over long distances, making it perfect for large outdoor areas, yards, and gardens.
  • Weatherproof Design: Built with weather-resistant enclosures to withstand rain, wind, and extreme temperatures for outdoor use.
  • High-Speed Wi-Fi: Supports high-speed Wi-Fi connections with dual or tri-band frequencies to provide stable connectivity.
  • Multiple Device Support: Ideal for connecting multiple outdoor devices, including cameras, smart lighting, and IoT devices.
  • Easy Setup: Typically features plug-and-play installation, with minimal configuration needed for quick deployment.

Components and Specifications

  • Wi-Fi Standard: Many models support Wi-Fi 5 (802.11ac) or Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) for faster and more reliable speeds.
  • Frequency Bands: Dual-band (2.4 GHz and 5 GHz) or tri-band for optimal performance and reduced congestion.
  • Weatherproof Enclosure: IP65 or higher rated to ensure protection from rain, dust, and environmental factors.
  • Ethernet Ports: Some models include Ethernet ports to connect wired devices outdoors for enhanced connectivity.
  • Power Supply: Can be powered via PoE (Power over Ethernet) for easier installation, especially in outdoor environments.
  • Signal Amplification: Built-in amplifiers to ensure strong and stable Wi-Fi coverage even at long distances.
