Wi-Fi 6 Extender (802.11ax)

Key Features

  • Wi-Fi 6 Technology: Supports the latest 802.11ax Wi-Fi standard, offering faster speeds, better efficiency, and improved capacity for more connected devices.
  • Higher Speeds: Wi-Fi 6 extenders provide speeds up to 9.6 Gbps, depending on the model, which is a significant improvement over previous Wi-Fi standards.
  • Better Performance in Crowded Areas: Designed to handle more devices simultaneously with technologies like MU-MIMO and OFDMA, which reduce congestion and latency.
  • Backward Compatibility: Fully compatible with older Wi-Fi standards (Wi-Fi 5, Wi-Fi 4), making it a versatile upgrade for any home or office network.
  • Enhanced Range: Improved coverage with better signal penetration, allowing users to extend Wi-Fi into previously weak or dead zones.

Components and Specifications

  • Wi-Fi Standard: 802.11ax (Wi-Fi 6), providing up to 4 times more capacity and faster speeds compared to Wi-Fi 5 (802.11ac).
  • Speeds: Speeds ranging from 1 Gbps to 9.6 Gbps, depending on the model and frequency bands supported (2.4 GHz and 5 GHz).
  • Compatibility: Works with Wi-Fi 5 and Wi-Fi 4 devices, ensuring seamless connectivity with older devices while providing the benefits of Wi-Fi 6 for newer devices.
  • OFDMA and MU-MIMO: Technologies that allow multiple devices to use the same channel simultaneously, reducing network congestion and improving performance.
  • Ethernet Ports: Many Wi-Fi 6 extenders feature one or more Ethernet ports for wired connections, ensuring stable internet access for gaming consoles, smart TVs, or desktop computers.
