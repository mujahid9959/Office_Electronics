Detachable Laptop (Tablet with Detachable Keyboard)

Key Features

  • Convertible Design: Functions as both a tablet and a laptop, with a detachable keyboard for flexible use.
  • Lightweight and Portable: Extremely compact and easy to carry, ideal for users who need mobility.
  • Touchscreen Display: Interactive touchscreen, often with pen support, enhances versatility for creative tasks and note-taking.
  • Battery Life: Generally provides extended battery life due to energy-efficient processors, allowing for all-day use.
  • Compatibility with Accessories: Supports a range of accessories like stylus pens, external keyboards, and docking stations.

Components and Specifications

  • Processor: Often equipped with Intel Core i3/i5/i7, Intel Atom, or ARM-based processors for efficient multitasking.
  • Memory (RAM): Typically ranges from 4GB to 16GB, providing smooth performance for basic tasks and moderate applications.
  • Storage: SSD storage options from 64GB to 512GB, optimized for faster boot times and app launches.
  • Graphics: Generally features integrated graphics, suitable for light graphics tasks and media consumption.
  • Display Size: Commonly between 10 to 13 inches with Full HD or higher resolution, offering clear visuals in a compact form.
  • Battery Life: Battery life varies by model, typically lasting 8 to 15 hours on a single charge.
  • Connectivity: Includes USB-C, USB-A, microSD, Wi-Fi 6, and Bluetooth options; some models support LTE connectivity.

Price Range

Price Range: $300 - $1,500+

Types of Detachable Laptops

  • Entry-Level Detachables: Budget-friendly options with basic specs, suitable for web browsing, media streaming, and note-taking.
  • Mid-Range Detachables: Offer improved processing power and RAM for multitasking, ideal for students and general office use.
  • High-End Detachables: Premium models with advanced specs, suitable for professionals needing versatility and power, including creative tasks.

Key Use Cases

  • On-the-Go Productivity: Ideal for users needing a lightweight device for work, meetings, and travel.
  • Note-Taking and Drawing: Perfect for students and artists, especially when used with a stylus for handwriting and sketching.
  • Media Consumption: Excellent for watching videos, reading, and web browsing, thanks to the portable form factor and touchscreen.
  • Education: Suitable for students who need a compact, versatile device for schoolwork and online learning.
