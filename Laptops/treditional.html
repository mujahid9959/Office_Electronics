Traditional/Clamshell Laptop

Key Features

  • Form Factor and Design: Compact clamshell design with a lid that opens to reveal a full keyboard and screen.
  • Portability: Lightweight and easy to carry, ideal for everyday use, work, or study.
  • Screen Size: Commonly ranges from 11 to 17 inches, with Full HD or 4K resolution options available in higher-end models.
  • Battery Life: Generally offers long battery life, ranging from 6 to 12 hours depending on the model and use case.
  • Performance: Varies from basic models with Intel Celeron/AMD Ryzen 3 processors to more powerful Core i5/i7 or Ryzen 5/7 CPUs.

Components and Specifications

  • Processor: From Intel Core i3 to Core i9, or AMD Ryzen 3 to Ryzen 9.
  • RAM: Typically 4GB to 16GB of RAM, with some models offering up to 32GB in high-end configurations.
  • Storage: SSDs are common, ranging from 128GB to 1TB, providing fast boot times and storage capacity.
  • Graphics: Integrated graphics for most models, but dedicated GPUs (e.g., NVIDIA GeForce) are available in gaming or workstation models.
  • Keyboard and Trackpad: Full-sized keyboard and precision trackpad, with optional backlighting in premium models.

Price Range

Price Range: $300 - $2,500+

Types of Traditional Laptops

  • Entry-Level: Basic computing tasks such as web browsing and document editing.
  • Mid-Range: Good for multitasking, light gaming, and media consumption.
  • High-End: Powerful laptops with high-end processors and graphics for gaming, video editing, and professional use.
  • Business/Professional: Often with added security features, better build quality, and extended warranty options.
