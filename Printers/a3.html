A3 MFPs (Multi-Function Printers)

Key Features

  • A3 Paper Size Support: A3 MFPs are capable of printing, scanning, and copying documents up to A3 size (11 x 17 inches), making them ideal for businesses requiring larger documents.
  • All-in-One Functionality: Combines printing, scanning, copying, and sometimes faxing in a single device for versatile office tasks.
  • High-Speed Printing: Designed to handle high-volume printing, providing fast output speeds for busy environments.
  • High-Quality Prints: Offers excellent print resolution, typically ranging from 1200 x 1200 dpi to 2400 x 2400 dpi, delivering sharp text and detailed images.
  • Large Paper Capacity: A3 MFPs usually come with larger paper trays to handle heavy printing demands, often supporting multiple paper sizes and types.

Components and Specifications

  • Print Speed: Typically ranges from 20 to 50 pages per minute (ppm), with higher-end models offering faster speeds for large print jobs.
  • Print Resolution: Usually 1200 x 1200 dpi or higher, ensuring crisp and clear text, as well as high-quality images and graphics.
  • Connectivity: Offers USB, Ethernet, and wireless connectivity options, enabling seamless integration into office networks and easy printing from multiple devices.
  • Scan Resolution: High scan resolutions of up to 1200 x 1200 dpi, ensuring detailed and accurate scanning of documents and images.
  • Paper Handling: Can handle paper sizes from A3, A4, A5, and more, with automatic duplex printing (double-sided) on select models.
  • Automatic Document Feeder (ADF): Many models come with an ADF for multi-page scanning, copying, and faxing, which boosts productivity.
