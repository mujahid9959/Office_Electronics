Dot Matrix MFPs (Multi-Function Printers)

Key Features

  • All-in-One Functionality: Dot Matrix MFPs combine printing, scanning, copying, and sometimes faxing in a single device.
  • Impact Printing: Uses impact printing technology, making it ideal for multi-part forms like invoices, receipts, and shipping labels.
  • Durability: Known for their long-lasting performance, Dot Matrix printers are designed for high-volume printing in demanding environments.
  • Low Cost of Operation: Dot Matrix printers are very cost-efficient in terms of running costs, particularly for bulk printing jobs.
  • Ribbon Usage: Uses a ribbon ink system, which is cost-effective and provides better value for high-volume printing needs.

Components and Specifications

  • Print Speed: Typically ranges from 300 to 500 characters per second (cps) for standard models, with higher speeds for advanced models.
  • Print Resolution: Ranges from 80 x 144 dpi to 240 x 144 dpi, suitable for printing clear text and basic graphics.
  • Connectivity: Offers USB, parallel, and serial connectivity options for integration into various business environments.
  • Scan Resolution: Typically ranges from 600 x 600 dpi to 1200 x 1200 dpi for scanning high-quality documents.
  • Paper Handling: Dot Matrix MFPs support continuous form paper, which is ideal for printing multi-part forms. They may also support cut-sheet paper handling.
  • Automatic Document Feeder (ADF): Some models come with ADF for batch scanning and copying of multiple pages.
