Multifunctional LED MFPs (Multi-Function Printers)

Key Features

  • LED Printing Technology: LED MFPs use LED lights instead of lasers for printing, offering faster speeds, less maintenance, and energy efficiency.
  • Compact Design: These MFPs are typically more compact compared to laser-based counterparts, making them ideal for small to medium-sized offices.
  • Multiple Functions: Capable of printing, scanning, copying, and in some cases, faxing. They help improve workflow efficiency by consolidating multiple functions into a single device.
  • Energy Efficient: LED technology uses less power, contributing to lower operating costs and less heat generation compared to traditional laser printers.
  • High-Resolution Output: High print resolutions (up to 1200 x 1200 dpi) and high-quality scanning options make LED MFPs suitable for professional-quality prints and documents.

Components and Specifications

  • Print Speed: LED MFPs can print at speeds ranging from 25 to 50 pages per minute (ppm), depending on the model.
  • Print Resolution: Typically, the print resolution ranges from 600 x 600 dpi to 1200 x 1200 dpi, offering crisp and sharp document output.
  • Paper Handling: They support a variety of paper sizes (A4, A3, Legal, Letter) and come with large paper trays to minimize reloading.
  • Connectivity: Most models feature wired (Ethernet, USB) and wireless (Wi-Fi, Mobile Printing) connectivity for easy sharing within office networks.
  • Monthly Duty Cycle: Suitable for mid to high-volume offices, with duty cycles often ranging from 30,000 to 75,000 pages per month.
