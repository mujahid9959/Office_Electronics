Laser MFPs (Multi-Function Printers)

Key Features

  • Multi-Functionality: Combines printing, scanning, copying, and sometimes faxing in one device, making it an all-in-one solution for offices.
  • Laser Printing Technology: Utilizes laser technology for high-quality, fast, and efficient printing, especially for text-heavy documents.
  • High-Speed Printing: Offers faster print speeds compared to inkjet printers, ideal for high-volume office use.
  • Energy Efficiency: Consumes less energy compared to inkjet printers, making it more environmentally friendly.
  • Durability and Reliability: Known for their long lifespan and ability to handle high-volume print jobs without frequent maintenance.

Components and Specifications

  • Print Speed: Typically ranges from 20 to 50 pages per minute (ppm) for monochrome models.
  • Resolution: Print resolution usually ranges from 600 x 600 dpi to 1200 x 1200 dpi, offering sharp text and high-quality prints.
  • Connectivity: Supports wired (USB, Ethernet) and wireless (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth) connections, providing flexibility in office environments.
  • Scan Capabilities: Scanning resolution can go up to 1200 x 1200 dpi, with features such as automatic document feeders (ADF) for quick scanning of multiple pages.
  • Paper Handling: Comes with multiple paper trays, with capacities ranging from 250 to 500 sheets, and often includes duplex (two-sided) printing.
  • Touchscreen Display: Many models feature user-friendly touchscreen interfaces for easy navigation and operation.
