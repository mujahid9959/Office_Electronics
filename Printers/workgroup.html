Workgroup MFPs (Multi-Function Printers)

Key Features

  • High-Volume Printing: Workgroup MFPs are designed for businesses and teams that require high-volume printing. These printers offer faster print speeds and higher duty cycles.
  • Multiple Functionality: These printers are all-in-one devices that support printing, scanning, copying, and sometimes faxing. Ideal for busy work environments.
  • Advanced Security Features: Workgroup MFPs typically include security features like secure printing, user authentication, and encryption to ensure the protection of sensitive information.
  • Network Connectivity: These MFPs support wired and wireless networking options, enabling easy access from multiple users across the network.
  • High-Resolution Scanning: Often includes scanning resolutions up to 1200 x 1200 dpi or higher, making them suitable for scanning high-quality documents and images.

Components and Specifications

  • Print Speed: Workgroup MFPs are designed for high-speed printing, with print speeds ranging from 30 pages per minute (ppm) to 70 ppm, depending on the model.
  • Print Resolution: Typically range from 600 x 600 dpi to 1200 x 1200 dpi, ensuring high-quality prints.
  • Paper Handling: These devices typically support a variety of paper sizes (A4, A3, Letter, Legal) and feature large paper trays to reduce the need for frequent reloading.
  • Connectivity: Ethernet, Wi-Fi, USB, and mobile printing support (Apple AirPrint, Google Cloud Print, etc.) allow for flexible connectivity and printing options.
  • Monthly Duty Cycle: Workgroup MFPs are built for high volumes, with monthly duty cycles often ranging from 30,000 to 100,000 pages or more.
