Portable MFPs (Multi-Function Printers)

Key Features

  • Compact and Lightweight: Portable MFPs are designed to be compact, lightweight, and easy to carry, making them ideal for remote workers, travelers, or small office setups.
  • All-in-One Functionality: Includes printing, scanning, and copying in a single portable device, offering convenience and efficiency on the go.
  • Wireless Connectivity: Most portable MFPs come with wireless features like Wi-Fi or Bluetooth for easy connection to smartphones, tablets, and laptops.
  • Battery-Powered Options: Some models come with built-in rechargeable batteries, allowing users to print and scan without the need for an electrical outlet.
  • Energy Efficient: These printers are designed to consume less power, making them an energy-efficient choice for mobile printing needs.

Components and Specifications

  • Print Speed: Usually lower than standard office MFPs, typically ranging from 5 to 15 pages per minute (ppm), depending on the model.
  • Print Resolution: Typically ranges from 600 x 600 dpi to 1200 x 1200 dpi for clear, crisp prints.
  • Connectivity Options: Wireless (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth), USB, and sometimes mobile printing apps support for seamless integration with various devices.
  • Battery Life: Battery-powered models can typically print up to 100-200 pages per charge, depending on the model and usage.
  • Paper Handling: Most portable MFPs support smaller paper sizes like A4, but some can also handle A3 and A5 paper sizes with smaller input trays.
