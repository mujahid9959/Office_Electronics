4K and Ultra HD Projectors

What Are 4K and Ultra HD Projectors?

4K and Ultra HD projectors deliver exceptional image quality with a resolution of 3840 x 2160 pixels (4K) or 1920 x 1080 pixels (Ultra HD). These projectors provide incredibly sharp, detailed, and vibrant visuals, making them the ideal choice for home theaters, gaming, and professional presentations. They offer superior picture clarity, color accuracy, and contrast compared to Full HD projectors.

Key Features

  • Resolution: 4K projectors offer a resolution of 3840 x 2160 pixels, while Ultra HD projectors deliver a resolution of 1920 x 1080 pixels. Both offer significantly sharper images than standard HD projectors.
  • Brightness: Most 4K and Ultra HD projectors come with brightness levels between 1,500 and 3,500 lumens, making them suitable for both home and office environments.
  • Color Accuracy: These projectors are designed to reproduce a wider color gamut, delivering more vivid and lifelike colors.
  • HDR Support: Many 4K and Ultra HD projectors now support High Dynamic Range (HDR) for even greater contrast and more detail in the brightest and darkest areas of an image.
  • Advanced Connectivity: These projectors feature multiple HDMI ports, USB ports, and often wireless capabilities for easy connection to streaming devices, laptops, or gaming consoles.

Specifications

  • Resolution: 4K (3840 x 2160) and Ultra HD (1920 x 1080).
  • Brightness: 1,500 to 3,500 lumens, depending on the model.
  • Contrast Ratio: High contrast ratios for improved picture quality, ranging from 10,000:1 to 100,000:1 or more.
  • Light Source: Laser, LED, or lamp-based light sources, with laser projectors offering longer lifespans and higher brightness.
  • HDR Support: Enhanced contrast and vibrant colors with HDR10 or Dolby Vision support on many models.
  • Connectivity: HDMI, USB, Bluetooth, and Wi-Fi options for flexible connectivity with various devices.

Price Range

Price Range: $1,500 - $20,000 (depending on resolution, features, and brand)
