Short-Throw and Ultra Short-Throw Projectors

What Are Short-Throw and Ultra Short-Throw Projectors?

Short-throw and ultra short-throw projectors are designed to project large images from a very short distance from the screen or wall. These projectors are ideal for small spaces, such as classrooms, offices, or home theaters, where there isn't much room for a traditional long-throw projector.

Short-Throw Projectors: Can project large images from a short distance (typically 3-8 feet). They are ideal for medium-sized rooms.

Ultra Short-Throw Projectors: Can project large images from very close distances (often just a few inches from the screen). These projectors are perfect for ultra-compact spaces like small conference rooms or home entertainment areas.

Key Features

  • Space-Saving Design: Short-throw and ultra short-throw projectors allow you to project large images without needing to place the projector far away from the screen.
  • Reduced Shadows: Since the projector is placed close to the screen, there's less chance of shadows being cast by people or objects in the room.
  • Large Image from a Short Distance: These projectors are designed to offer big-screen projections even in small rooms.
  • Flexible Placement: These projectors can be placed on a shelf, mounted on the wall, or even in front of the screen, providing flexibility for small spaces.
  • Ideal for Interactive Presentations: Ultra short-throw projectors are often used in interactive whiteboards, classrooms, or collaborative workspaces due to their ability to project in close proximity.

Specifications

  • Resolution: Available in HD (1080p), 4K, and 720p models, depending on the projector.
  • Brightness: Typically between 2,000 and 4,000 lumens, offering bright images in both light and dark environments.
  • Throw Ratio: Short-throw projectors have a throw ratio of 0.4 to 1.0, while ultra short-throw projectors have a throw ratio of 0.25 or lower.
  • Connectivity: HDMI, USB, VGA, and wireless options (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and screen mirroring) for flexible connection to devices.
  • Lens Shift: Some models support lens shift for easy alignment, allowing for precise image positioning.
  • Sound System: Many models feature built-in speakers for enhanced audio, but you can also connect external sound systems for better quality.

Price Range

Price Range: $500 - $3,500 (depending on features, resolution, brightness, and brand)
