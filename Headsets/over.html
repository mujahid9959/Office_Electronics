Over-Ear Headsets (Circumaural)

What Are Over-Ear Headsets (Circumaural)?

Over-ear headsets, also known as circumaural headsets, are a type of headphone designed to fully cover the ears. These headsets typically provide superior sound quality and comfort, as they have larger drivers and padding that creates a seal around the ears, enhancing sound isolation. They are ideal for extended listening sessions and provide excellent audio performance for gaming, music, and other media consumption.

Key Features

  • Excellent Sound Quality: Large drivers provide deep bass and clear high frequencies.
  • Enhanced Comfort: Soft, plush ear pads fully surround the ears for a comfortable fit during extended use.
  • Superior Noise Isolation: The closed-back design blocks out external noise, making them great for immersive listening experiences.
  • Ideal for Home Use: While less portable than on-ear models, they offer a premium listening experience for at-home use.
  • Wireless Options: Many models come with Bluetooth for wireless connectivity, eliminating cables for more freedom.
  • Built-in Microphone: Perfect for hands-free calling, gaming, or voice assistants.

Specifications

  • Driver Type: Typically dynamic or planar magnetic drivers for superior sound.
  • Frequency Response: 20 Hz - 20 kHz (varies by model).
  • Impedance: Usually 32 ohms to 80 ohms (ideal for both portable and high-powered devices).
  • Bluetooth Range: 30 feet (10 meters) for wireless models.
  • Battery Life: 15-30 hours (for wireless models).
  • Microphone: Noise-canceling or omnidirectional microphone for clear voice communication.
