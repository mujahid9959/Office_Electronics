DECT Headsets

What Are DECT Headsets?

DECT (Digital Enhanced Cordless Telecommunications) headsets are wireless communication devices commonly used in office environments. They are designed to provide high-quality audio and range, offering users the flexibility to move around while maintaining a stable connection with their phone systems or computers. These headsets are primarily used for professional calls, conference calls, and VoIP communications.

Key Features

  • Wireless Connectivity: Uses DECT technology for stable, long-range wireless communication without the interference often associated with Bluetooth.
  • Long Battery Life: DECT headsets offer longer battery life, typically lasting up to 8-12 hours of continuous talk time.
  • Wide Range: Range typically up to 100 meters, allowing users to roam freely within an office environment.
  • Noise-Canceling Microphone: Equipped with noise-canceling microphones to filter out background noise and ensure clear communication.
  • Comfortable Fit: Designed for all-day comfort with adjustable headbands and cushioned ear cups.
  • Easy Integration: Seamlessly integrates with office phones, computers, or VoIP systems via base stations or direct connections.

Specifications

  • Connectivity: DECT wireless connection (up to 100 meters range)
  • Battery Life: 8 to 12 hours of talk time (depending on the model)
  • Charging Time: Approximately 1 to 2 hours for a full charge
  • Microphone: Noise-canceling, adjustable boom microphone
  • Audio Quality: Wideband audio for clear voice communication
  • Weight: Lightweight, typically around 80 to 150 grams
  • Wearing Style: Over-the-head, over-the-ear, or ear-hook designs
