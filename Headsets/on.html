On-Ear Headsets (Supra-Aural)

What Are On-Ear Headsets (Supra-Aural)?

On-ear headsets, also known as supra-aural headsets, are a type of headphones that sit directly on top of your ears. Unlike over-ear headsets, they do not completely cover the ear but provide a comfortable listening experience for short to medium listening sessions. These headsets are compact and portable, making them ideal for on-the-go use while offering a decent sound quality for their size.

Key Features

  • Comfortable Fit: Lightweight design with padded ear cups for extended use.
  • Portable: Compact and foldable design for easy storage and transport.
  • Good Sound Quality: Balanced audio with clear highs and mids, though less bass-heavy than over-ear options.
  • Noise Isolation: Helps block out some ambient noise due to the snug fit.
  • Wireless Options: Many models come with Bluetooth for wireless connectivity.
  • Durability: Built with robust materials for long-lasting performance.

Specifications

  • Driver Type: Dynamic drivers for balanced sound.
  • Frequency Response: 20 Hz - 20 kHz (varies by model).
  • Impedance: Usually around 32 ohms (ideal for portable devices).
  • Bluetooth Range: Typically 30 feet (10 meters).
  • Battery Life: 10-20 hours (for wireless models).
  • Microphone: Built-in mic for hands-free calls and voice commands.
