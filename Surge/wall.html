Wall-Mount Surge Protectors

Overview

Wall-mount surge protectors are surge protection devices designed to be mounted directly on the wall, providing a clean, space-saving solution for protecting electronic devices from power surges and spikes. These protectors are typically used in areas where power strips might be inconvenient, or where you need to provide surge protection in a fixed location, such as behind a television, home office desk, or entertainment center. Wall-mount surge protectors can offer multiple outlets and often come with additional features like USB charging ports and high-energy protection to safeguard your valuable electronics.

Price Range: $20 - $80

Features

  • Space-Saving Design: Wall-mount surge protectors are designed to be mounted directly on the wall, eliminating the need for bulky power strips and providing a cleaner look in your space.
  • Surge Protection: These devices offer essential surge protection to safeguard your electronics and appliances from damage caused by electrical spikes or surges.
  • Multiple Outlets: Wall-mount surge protectors typically feature multiple outlets (4-6 or more), allowing you to connect multiple devices at once, while keeping cords neatly organized and out of sight.
  • USB Charging Ports: Some models come with integrated USB ports to allow you to conveniently charge your devices, such as smartphones, tablets, and other USB-powered electronics, without the need for additional adapters.
  • Heavy Duty Protection: Many wall-mount surge protectors feature high joule ratings, meaning they can absorb larger surges of electricity and protect your devices more effectively.
  • Indicator Lights: Some models come with indicator lights that show the status of the surge protector, ensuring itâ€™s working correctly and providing adequate protection.
  • Wall-Mounting Hardware Included: These surge protectors come with all the necessary hardware to securely mount the unit to the wall, providing a quick and easy installation process.
  • Safety Certifications: Many wall-mount surge protectors are UL-listed or meet other safety standards, ensuring they are reliable and safe to use in your home or office.

Applications

  • Home Entertainment Systems: Wall-mount surge protectors are ideal for protecting home theater systems, gaming consoles, and media players while keeping cords organized and off the floor.
  • Home Offices: These surge protectors can be mounted near your desk to provide easy access to power for computers, printers, and other office equipment.
  • Living Rooms and Bedrooms: Place a wall-mount surge protector behind furniture or in the corner of a room to protect TVs, sound systems, and charging devices.
  • Outdoor Use (Covered Areas): Some models are designed for outdoor use in covered areas, such as patios, providing surge protection for outdoor lights, fans, or other devices.
  • Kitchen or Workshop: Mount a surge protector on the wall in a kitchen or workshop to protect appliances like blenders, microwaves, and power tools from electrical spikes.

Conclusion

Wall-mount surge protectors are an excellent choice for anyone looking for a convenient, space-saving solution to protect their electronics and appliances from power surges. With prices ranging from $20 for basic models to $80+ for high-capacity units with extra features like USB ports and heavy-duty protection, wall-mount surge protectors are an affordable way to enhance the safety and longevity of your devices while maintaining a clean and organized space. Whether used in home offices, entertainment centers, or kitchens, these surge protectors provide reliable, full-home protection in an easy-to-install design.
