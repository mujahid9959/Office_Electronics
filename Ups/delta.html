Delta Conversion Online UPS

Overview

The Delta Conversion Online UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) is an advanced power protection solution that combines the benefits of Double Conversion and Delta Conversion technology. This UPS provides the highest level of protection by ensuring continuous, clean, and stable power without any interruptions. Unlike traditional online UPS systems, Delta Conversion Online UPS units offer superior efficiency and lower heat generation, making them an ideal choice for critical applications such as data centers, industrial equipment, healthcare facilities, and telecommunications.

Price Range: $1,000 - $30,000

Features

  • Delta Conversion Technology: The Delta Conversion Online UPS incorporates a unique Delta Conversion architecture that provides higher efficiency while maintaining constant voltage regulation. This technology reduces energy loss and minimizes heat generation.
  • High Efficiency: With an efficiency rate of up to 96%, Delta Conversion Online UPS units offer significant energy savings compared to traditional online UPS systems.
  • Continuous Power Supply: The Delta Conversion Online UPS continuously supplies power to critical loads, ensuring that connected equipment is always protected from power interruptions, fluctuations, and noise.
  • Advanced Battery Management: These systems include intelligent battery management features, which optimize battery life and provide timely alerts when battery maintenance or replacement is needed.
  • Compact Design: The compact design of Delta Conversion Online UPS units makes them space-efficient while maintaining robust power protection, suitable for data centers and industrial applications.
  • Real-time Monitoring: Some models feature LCD displays or remote monitoring capabilities that allow users to track power usage, battery status, and operational conditions in real time.
  • Low Total Cost of Ownership: With improved energy efficiency and lower maintenance needs, Delta Conversion Online UPS systems offer a low total cost of ownership over their lifespan.
  • Long Battery Life: These UPS units are designed to extend battery life with features like intelligent charging and temperature-compensated charging to protect the battery from damage and ensure longer service life.
