Ferroresonant UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply)

Overview

Ferroresonant UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) systems are a type of online UPS technology that uses ferroresonant transformers to provide stable and continuous power to connected devices. These systems are designed to provide excellent voltage regulation and are known for their ability to handle large load changes without affecting the output power. Ferroresonant UPS units are widely used in environments where consistent power quality is critical, such as in industrial settings, medical equipment, and sensitive electronic systems. They offer robust protection against power outages, sags, and surges.

Price Range: $500 - $10,000

Features

  • Ferroresonant Transformer: Ferroresonant UPS units use a specialized transformer to regulate voltage and ensure the power supply remains stable, even during power fluctuations or load changes.
  • Superior Voltage Regulation: Ferroresonant UPS units provide excellent voltage regulation, making them ideal for sensitive equipment that requires consistent, clean power.
  • High Surge Capacity: These systems are capable of handling high surge loads and transient spikes, making them suitable for environments where equipment may experience sudden changes in load.
  • Continuous Power Supply: Ferroresonant UPS units provide continuous power to critical loads, ensuring that connected devices remain protected from power disturbances, outages, or electrical noise.
  • Low Harmonic Distortion: These systems are designed to minimize harmonic distortion in the output power, ensuring that connected equipment operates smoothly without electrical interference.
  • Durable and Reliable: Ferroresonant UPS units are known for their long lifespan and durability, making them an excellent investment for applications requiring long-term, reliable power protection.
  • Energy-Efficient: Modern Ferroresonant UPS systems are designed to be more energy-efficient, reducing operating costs and minimizing wasted energy during normal operation.
  • Cost-Effective: Ferroresonant UPS units typically have a lower initial cost compared to other types of online UPS systems, making them a cost-effective solution for small to medium-sized installations.

  • Telecommunications: Ferroresonant UPS systems are used in telecommunications networks to prevent downtime and protect critical communication infrastructure from power disruptions.
  • Data Centers: Ferroresonant UPS units are commonly deployed in data centers to ensure reliable power to servers, networking equipment, and other vital systems, reducing the risk of data loss and downtime.
  • Laboratories: In laboratory settings where precise equipment is used, Ferroresonant UPS systems ensure that sensitive instruments are protected from power surges, sags, and outages.
