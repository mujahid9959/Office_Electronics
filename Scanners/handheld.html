Handheld Scanners

Key Features

  • Portable: Handheld scanners are lightweight and compact, making them ideal for on-the-go scanning, whether in the office or traveling.
  • Easy to Use: With a simple design and intuitive interface, handheld scanners are user-friendly and easy to operate, even for first-time users.
  • Battery-Powered: Many handheld scanners are battery-operated, allowing you to use them without being tethered to a power outlet.
  • Convenience: Perfect for scanning receipts, business cards, and documents without the need for a large, stationary scanner.
  • Variety of Uses: Can be used to scan text documents, images, and even barcodes or QR codes for a variety of purposes.

Specifications

  • Resolution: Usually between 300 dpi to 600 dpi for clear document scanning.
  • Connectivity: Can connect via USB, Bluetooth, or Wi-Fi depending on the model.
  • Battery Life: Typically 200-300 scans per charge for most models, depending on battery size.
  • File Formats: Supports various file formats such as PDF, JPEG, and TIFF.
  • Scan Speed: Usually around 5-10 pages per minute (ppm), depending on the model and quality settings.
