Wide-Format Scanners

Key Features

  • Large Format Scanning: Wide-format scanners are designed to scan documents larger than standard letter-sized pages, making them ideal for architectural plans, blueprints, posters, and engineering drawings.
  • High Resolution: These scanners offer high resolution (up to 2400 dpi or higher), ensuring precise capture of intricate details and fine lines in large documents.
  • Speed: Wide-format scanners are typically fast, capable of scanning large documents in seconds or minutes, depending on the model.
  • Advanced Image Correction: Many models include features like color enhancement, image correction, and noise reduction, providing high-quality scans.
  • Multiple Connectivity Options: Wide-format scanners often come with USB, Ethernet, and Wi-Fi options for seamless integration into various workflows.

Specifications

  • Resolution: Ranges from 1200 dpi to 2400 dpi for high-detail scans.
  • Scan Area: Typically up to A1 or larger formats (up to 36 inches wide or more).
  • Scan Speed: Varies from 1-5 seconds per page depending on resolution and document size.
  • Software Integration: Many models come with software for image enhancement, file management, and OCR.
  • Connectivity: USB, Ethernet, Wi-Fi, and sometimes cloud integration for flexibility in professional settings.
