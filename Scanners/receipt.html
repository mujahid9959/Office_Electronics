Receipt Scanners

Key Features

  • High Speed: Receipt scanners offer fast scanning speeds, allowing you to process multiple receipts in a short period.
  • Compact Design: These scanners are often small and portable, making them perfect for small business owners or individuals on the go.
  • OCR (Optical Character Recognition): Includes built-in OCR technology that converts scanned receipts into editable and searchable text.
  • Versatile Paper Handling: Many models support scanning receipts of different sizes, from small paper slips to larger receipts.
  • Automatic Document Feeder (ADF): Some models include ADF to quickly scan multiple receipts at once, improving workflow efficiency.

Specifications

  • Resolution: Typically 600 DPI for clear and accurate scans of receipts.
  • Connectivity: USB 2.0/3.0 and sometimes wireless options like Bluetooth or Wi-Fi for easy integration with other devices.
  • OCR Support: Built-in OCR software to extract text from receipts for easy data entry or record keeping.
  • Scan Speed: Often ranging from 20 to 40 pages per minute, depending on the model and resolution.
  • Receipt Size Support: Works with a variety of receipt sizes, typically from 3" to 8" in length.
