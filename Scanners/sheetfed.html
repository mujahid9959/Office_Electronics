Sheetfed Scanners

Key Features

  • Fast Scanning: Sheetfed scanners excel in high-speed scanning, making them ideal for bulk document scanning in offices and businesses.
  • Compact Design: These scanners are usually smaller and more portable compared to flatbed scanners, saving space on desks or in work environments.
  • Automatic Document Feeder (ADF): Equipped with an ADF, sheetfed scanners can automatically feed multiple pages into the scanner without manual intervention.
  • High-Volume Scanning: They are designed for scanning large volumes of paper quickly, which is perfect for businesses needing to process lots of documents.
  • Multi-Page Scanning: Capable of scanning multi-page documents in one go, saving time in environments where high efficiency is crucial.

Components and Specifications

  • Resolution: Sheetfed scanners typically offer resolutions from 600 dpi to 1200 dpi, providing clarity and accuracy for text and graphics.
  • Scan Speed: Sheetfed scanners can scan up to 60 pages per minute (ppm), depending on the model and document type.
  • ADF Capacity: Most sheetfed scanners feature an automatic document feeder (ADF) with capacities ranging from 20 to 100 pages.
  • Connectivity: Sheetfed scanners come with USB 3.0, USB 2.0, or wireless options for easy connection to computers and networks.
  • Size and Capacity: These scanners support standard document sizes such as letter (8.5” x 11”), A4, and legal (8.5” x 14”), with some models offering larger size options.
