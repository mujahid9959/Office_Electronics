Portable Scanners

Key Features

  • Compact & Portable: These scanners are lightweight and compact, making them perfect for users who need to scan documents on the go.
  • USB Powered: Most portable scanners are powered through USB, eliminating the need for an external power supply and making them easy to use with laptops or other devices.
  • Quick Scanning: Despite their small size, portable scanners can offer decent scan speeds, typically scanning a page in 5-10 seconds.
  • Simple Connectivity: Portable scanners often feature USB 2.0 or USB 3.0 connectivity, allowing for quick and easy integration with a variety of devices.
  • Wireless Options: Some models offer Bluetooth or Wi-Fi capabilities, enabling scanning without the need for direct cable connections.

Components and Specifications

  • Resolution: Portable scanners typically provide resolutions ranging from 600 dpi to 1200 dpi, ideal for capturing high-quality scans.
  • Scan Speed: Most portable scanners offer a scan speed of 5-10 seconds per page, depending on the model and settings.
  • Connectivity: USB 2.0 or USB 3.0 ports are standard, while some devices offer wireless capabilities like Bluetooth or Wi-Fi for easier use with mobile devices.
  • Size: The compact design of portable scanners allows them to easily fit in a backpack, briefcase, or drawer.
  • Battery Life: Some portable scanners feature rechargeable batteries for scanning when no power outlet is available.
