Duplex Scanners

Key Features

  • Two-Sided Scanning: Duplex scanners can scan both sides of a document at once, improving efficiency and reducing scanning time.
  • High-Speed Scanning: Duplex scanners are designed to handle high-volume scanning tasks, providing fast scan speeds for both sides of a page in one pass.
  • Automatic Document Feeder (ADF): Most duplex scanners come equipped with an ADF, allowing for hands-free scanning of multiple pages at once.
  • Compact Design: Despite offering duplex scanning, these scanners remain compact enough for small office or home use.
  • Advanced Features: Many duplex scanners include features such as OCR (Optical Character Recognition), image enhancement, and integration with document management systems.

Specifications

  • Resolution: Duplex scanners typically offer resolutions of 600 dpi or higher, suitable for professional-grade scanning.
  • Scan Speed: Duplex scanners generally offer faster scanning speeds, ranging from 20 to 60 pages per minute (ppm), depending on the model.
  • Connectivity: USB 2.0/3.0 or Wi-Fi options are common for duplex scanners, ensuring easy connection to computers and networks.
  • Paper Size Compatibility: Most duplex scanners handle various paper sizes, including A4, legal, and even business cards or receipts.
  • Software Integration: Many duplex scanners come bundled with software for image editing, file management, and OCR capabilities.
